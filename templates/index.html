What is PicoTale?
×

Responsive Down to Mobile

PictoTale is an ebook reader, which will go through an uploaded ebook paragraph wise. Per paragraph it will present a AI-generated picture of the descriptive tests of the paragraph and read out the texts of all conversations found there.

  • Built on Python, Flask and HTML5/CSS3/JS
  • Pictures AI-generated by Stable Diffusion
  • Text-to-Speech done by gTTS
  • All sources of the project are AI-generated with the help of ChatGPT4 and BING-Chat

Upload a new book as pdf-file and let PictoTale take you on the trip through AI-generated pictures and listen to the conversations of the characters of the story.

Browse through the books which are already uploaded and available. Select the book you want to view & listen to in PictoTale.
